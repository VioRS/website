I make videos sometimes.

I started off in 2006 with Hypercam and Windows Movie Maker, and worked my way up to better NLVEs like Sony Vegas Pro (I didn't pay for it).

I mostly make YouTube videos for Old-School RuneScape, an online MMO where I'm the longest-running video-maker in the community. I've also made some Minecraft videos and a few other things over the years.

Reign of Terror (2018)

A documentary on the controversial and infamous RuneScape clan "Reign of Terror" - written, narrated, filmed and edited by myself (though I wish I'd found someone else to do the narration).

This video gained over 50,000 views.

The Wilderness - An Unathorised Biography (2012)

A documentary on the PvP aspect of RuneScape circa 2012 - written and narrated by Eugene Marshall; filmed and edited by myself.

This video gained almost 150,000 views and over 10,000 likes, and RuneScape historian "July" names it as his favourite RuneScape video on YouTube.

Can I Have 10k? (2011)
❗ Strong language warning.

A machinima-style video for a "beat poem" - written, read, filmed and edited by myself. It's a criticism of the game (RuneScape)'s materialistic and wealth-driven community at the time.

This video gained over 300,000 views.
